At linktrail, we want to build your one-stop destination for online research. With our first product linktrail, we created an environment for people like you to find relevant resources that are linked to another real person, because search results alone are not capable of satisfying highly specific information needs. Moreover, we believe that the most important purpose of the Internet is linking people to other people.

For these reasons, we have designed an online environment that offers a truly new way for people to store, manage and share their favourite online resources and enables them to engage in long-lasting relationships of collaboration and trust with other users.
