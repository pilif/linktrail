Linktrail Inc. is an Internet start-up company founded in August of 2000 by six enthusiastic entrepreneurs in Zurich, Switzerland.

Linktrail Inc. is based in the city of Zurich, Switzerland. With its beautiful lake and the view of the distant alps, the city is an inspiring place to live and work.

Linktrail Inc.'s offices are located in the middle of the financial district, at a two minute walk from the lake.
